Ready to Race: KTM's official tagline and philosophy.
Orange: The signature, bold color of the brand.
Performance: A core value emphasizing high-end power and speed.
Extreme: A word used to describe the limits pushed by KTM's motorcycles.
Adventure: A key concept for its long-distance and off-road bikes.
Purity: A brand value signifying a focus on pure function over unnecessary design elements.

KTM AG is an Austrian motorcycle manufacturer known for its high-performance street and off-road bikes. Founded in 1953, KTM is famous for its slogan “Ready to Race”.

Popular models include the Duke, RC, and Adventure series, combining power, style, and innovation for riders worldwide.
